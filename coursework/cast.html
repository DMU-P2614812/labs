Juhan Ulfsak

Juhan Ulfsak was born on April 18, 1973 in Estonia. He is an actor and director, known for Tenet (2020), Doktor Stockmann (1989) and The Bank (2018).

Robert Pattinson

Robert Thomas Pattinson was born on May 13, 1986 in London. He is an actor, known for Twilight (2008 - 2012) Tenet (2020), and the future Batman (2022) which everyone seems really excited about.

John Washington

John Washington was born on July 28, 1984 in Los Angeles. He is an actor, known for Tenet (2020), and the future Malcom & Marie (released in February 2021).
